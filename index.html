Biography

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the College of Computer Science and Engineering at Hunan University. Prior to this, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow in the School of Data Science at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-SZ), working with Prof. Yixiang Fang. During my time there, I also had the privilege of collaborating with Prof. Chenhao Ma and the highly talented PhD candidate Yingli Zhou.

My general research interests mainly focus on the areas of data management, data mining, and artificial intelligence over big data, particularly big graph data.

Research Interests

My general research interests mainly focus on the areas of data management, data mining, and artificial intelligence over big data, particularly big graph data. Currently, I am working on the following research topics:

  • Graph data: cohesive subgraph search, subgraph maintenance
  • Parallel computing: cohesive subgraph decomposition, matrix multiplication
